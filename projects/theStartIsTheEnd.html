TheStartIsTheEnd

A Complex laser stratergy game where player could go crazy even on one mistake.

Description

A Game where the ultimate quest is to find the start button itself, even though it looks very simple its not actually the positions of each lasers matter a inch could make you loose tens of minutes. This was the first game i made with a team but as there was not much of time and our tastes were different we werent able to do a very great job, but its still a good game can play for atleast one time and have fun.

  • Platform: Web (WebGL)
  • Engine: Unity
  • Status: Released
