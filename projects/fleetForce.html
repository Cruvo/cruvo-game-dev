Fleet Force

Very simple top-down game where player has to defend by destroying enemy ships.

Description

Fleet Force is a simple game where the player has to destroy all the enemy ships shooting the cannon in from of the player ship while avoiding enemy's bullets and colliding with their ships.

  • Genre: Casual
  • Platform: Web (WebGL)
  • Engine: Unity
  • Status: Released
